Manifesto

“As we look ahead into the next century, leaders will be those who empower others.” -Bill Gates

WWe empower the ‘ordinaires’: chef-preneurs with unrealized dreams of sharing their love and knowledge of everything food. We unleash the wealth of untapped creativity, skills and knowledge by empowering foodies. Extraordinary food experiences will be created: dinners in total darkness or total silence, all in red, co-creating a meal, a private Chopped-event… Created by the masses for the masses.

Foodie

a person with an ardent or refined interest in food. Seeks new food experiences as a hobby rather than simply eating out of hunger.

Chef-prenuer

a Foodie wanting to make a business of his/her passion for cooking and being a chef.

value proposition
For amateur chefs who need access to an affordable professional kitchen and exposure to customers, Foodtopia is a physical and online platform helping them build a viable business. Unlike Feastly and Green Chef, Foodtopia offers rising chefs a maker space and an interactive customer lab so they can build their brand.

Food Explorer

a Foodie constantly hunting down new ways to experience food. A consumer or audience of food experiences.
